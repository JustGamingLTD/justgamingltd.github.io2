PVAI-Lore-History-Ewoikio

Summary

Ewoikio is a minor power on an island near the continent of Siniath Dór. It is an isolated nation that remains quite undeveloped.

Location

The kingdom of Ewoikio lies on an island near Siniath Dór, the continent that would later be invaded by the Invaders. Because it is an island nation, it remained isolated far longer than any other nation on Siniath Dór, and was never invaded. It, however, remained undeveloped, even until the Invasion.

Politics

Ewoikio is a neutral kingdom. It was isolationist, and remained so until the Invasion.

History

Not much is known about the early history of Ewoikio. It is known that they had a civil war around a century before the Invasion. During the Invasion, the kindom wasn't completely destroyed by the rift, allowing it to survive the initial Invasion, and was later defended by the Alliance, in the Battle of Ewoikio.
