PVAI-Lore-History-Leolia

Summary

Leolia is one of the minor powers on the continent of Siniath Dór. It was a neutral nation.

Location

The kingdom of Leolia lies in the middle of Siniath Dór, the continent that would later be invaded by the Invaders. It shares a border with Aquania and Herkalion, both marked by a river. The rivers protected it from both Heraklion and Aquania.

Politics

Leolia was a neutral nation and a sort of buffer zone between the kingdoms of Heraklion and Aquania.

History

Leolia formed around the same time Aquania and Heraklion formed. It wasn't ever involved in any war, and its citizens fully supported this neutrality.
