PVAI-Lore-Ships-Black Dragon

The Black Dragon was the flagship of RAGTHERBOS, the leader of the Invader's airforce. It was a one of a kind ship, which was the most powerful in existance, having 10 canons and being huge. The ship was involved in the Battle of the Fates, but was destroyed during it, by Elytra Wing Strike Force, and RAGTHERBOS was killed as well.
