PVAI-Lore

PVAI has a detailed history with extensive lore, from the moment the invasion started, to the history of every single map. All the characters, ship classes and squads are described. Every map connects to the larger story, so you feel every map you play has impact on the rest of the server. If you don't want to read all of the lore, you can just read the Basic History of PVAI, which covers everything you need to know to fully enjoy PVAI.
