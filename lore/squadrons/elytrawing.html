PVAI-Lore-Battles-Elytra Wing Strike Force

Elytra Wing Strike Force is one of the most elite squadrons of the Alliance. It usually operates from a Raider Class Airship, using it to dispatch their troops to heavily guared and fortified locations. They participated in the Battle of the Fates, and killed RAGTHERBOS, allowing the Alliance's fleet to win the battle.
