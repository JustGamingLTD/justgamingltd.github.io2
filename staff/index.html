Staff Ranks Explained

Owner

The owner owns the server. He is all powerfull and decides most large scale things about the server.

The Co-Owner helps the owner, he helps deciding things and with all other kinds of things.

Moderator

A moderator is tasked with keeping order in the server. You can tell them to inspect users you suspect are hackers and similar things.
